The event

Sillicon Valley comes to HackaSoton is a 48 hour weekend for Europe's brightest developers, designers and enterpreneurs to get together create things no-one can even imagine!

Awesome Speakers, Incredible Mentors, 3D printers, Google Glasses, Free Food and Drink - We will have every essential tool you will need to achieve the impossible: Create a fully functional, scalable, and monetizable StartUp in less than 48 hours.

Crazy? Maybe! Impossible? Not even close! Many individuals created Revolutionary Concepts at our past Startup Weekend in Southampton, Incredible StartUps at our Startup Weekend in Slovenia and Mind Blowing Hacks at our Summerhacks in Romania.

This is your opportunity to take the leap into the amazing world of StartUps. Come join us in this amazing experience - we assure you that you won't regret it!

Main Sponsor

We would like to give our most sincere thanks to Bloomberg LP. for providing thorough support on helping us promote entrepreneurship throughout communities in the UK! They enable us to provide our attendees the experience they deserve. We are truly honoured for having their support, as they are extremely active in the young entrepreneurial communities.





Golden Sponsors

Our most sincere thanks to the University of Southampton for their help throughout all our events in Southampton, Slovenia, and Romania - they have provided great support since our first events.



Entrepreneur First is an incredible, intense programme where they support, mentor and guide exceptional graduates to build their own high-growth startups. This network of passionate entrepreneurs has had a huge positive impact in the entrepreneurial communities in the UK.







Partners

The SVC2UK are the heroes of entrepreneurship in the UK - This organization is made up of the most influential and brilliant evangelists in the Silicon Valley. They have been supporting the young entrepreneurial communities for several years now. Without them, this event won't be possible - we really appreciate their support!





Fish On Toast is the Entrepreneurial Society of the University of Southampton. HackaSoton and Fish on Toast work together in many projects and share the same objective of connecting engineers with entrepreneurs to make the impossible happen.
